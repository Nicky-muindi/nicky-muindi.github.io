Privacy Policy for AI GPT Video & Image Generator

Last updated: December 4, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how AI GPT Video & Image Generator, owned by Hillary Kipyegon, handles user data and protects your privacy.

1. No Personal Data Collected

This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information such as your name, email address, or location. Your privacy is our top priority.

2. AI Generation via Secure APIs and Local AI within the APP

All image and video generation is processed through trusted third-party AI APIs. Text prompts you provide are sent directly to the API provider for generation and are not stored or analyzed by us.

3. Local Processing

Generated media is processed in real time and stored only on your device. Nothing is uploaded to our servers unless you choose to share it.

4. No Tracking or Analytics

We do not use analytics tools, cookies, or tracking technologies. No behavioral or usage data is collected.

5. Permissions

This app requests limited permissions necessary for functionality:

  • Internet: To access AI generation APIs.
  • Storage (optional): Only if you choose to save generated images or videos.

6. Third-Party Services

We use reputable AI and ad service providers. Their respective privacy policies apply during use of their APIs or ads. We ensure all third parties comply with strong privacy standards.

7. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed to children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Updates to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the new policy.
