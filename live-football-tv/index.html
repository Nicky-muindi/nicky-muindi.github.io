About the App

Football Coverage in One Place

Live Football TV is a football-focused app designed to help users stay informed with match updates, fixtures, schedules, and football-related content from leagues around the world.

The app is built with simplicity and accessibility in mind, allowing fans to follow football activity without unnecessary complexity.
